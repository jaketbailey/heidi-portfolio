Personal profile

I am an outgoing, approachable, enthusiastic, self-motivated graduate and employee, who has just graduated with degree in Journalism at the University of Sheffield. In addition to my studies, I have gained extensive experience using Adobe Premiere Pro in filming, video/audio editing throughout my Btec in creative Media and my Journalism degree. In addition, I have advanced creative skills throughout a variety of projects I have taken part in including writing and planning feature articles and news articles for a magazine brand.

I invest my spare time in my local community through my volunteering work at Burnt Tree Croft Care Home by supporting the vulnerable. During Covid-19 I have maintained support through regular communication with elderly residents via video, phone and letters. Christmas 2020 I volunteered as a ‘Christmas caller’ at Ignite Imaginations phoning the isolated elderly on Christmas Day.

My time management strength has allowed me to manage my studies, volunteering and my role as an office and marketing assistant. I am very versatile and adaptable to situations and manage problem-solving with ease.

As a committed individual, I have always taken pride in my 100% dedication to everything I do. An example of this is my membership of Sheffield Swing Dance Society. I am involved with a diverse age and range of people, allowing me to build upon my existing experience working, interacting, and communicating effectively as part of a team.

Before Covid-19 in ‘open mic’ performances, I regular participated as a solo singer and poetry performer, utilising and developing my presentational skills in front of a live audience.
